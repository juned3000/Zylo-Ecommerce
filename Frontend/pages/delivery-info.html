Important Information

Delivery Delays

  • Monsoon season may cause 1-2 days additional delay
  • Festival periods may extend delivery by 2-3 days
  • Natural disasters or strikes may affect delivery
  • Remote areas may take longer due to limited transportation

Delivery Attempts

  • We make 3 delivery attempts before returning the package
  • You'll be notified before each delivery attempt
  • Package held at local facility for 7 days after failed attempts
  • Return shipping charges apply if package is returned

Delivery Requirements

  • Valid ID proof required for high-value orders (>Rs. 10,000)
  • Someone must be available to receive the package
  • Age verification required for certain products
  • Clear address and working phone number mandatory

Packaging

  • Eco-friendly and recyclable packaging materials
  • Bubble wrap and protective covering for fragile items
  • Tamper-proof packaging for high-value items
  • Special gift wrapping available during checkout
